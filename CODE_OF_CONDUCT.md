# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Maestro Deck to be a welcoming place for anyone who wants to build, use, or contribute to it — regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in code reviews, issues, and discussions.
- Assume good intent. When you disagree, focus on the technical point, not the person.
- Help newcomers. Everyone was new at something once.
- Credit prior work and contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or hijacking discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of project communication channels.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code, email
**blueshork.dev@gmail.com**. Reports are handled in confidence.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or revoke contribution privileges
for behavior that violates this code. Decisions are documented in the relevant
issue or PR thread when possible.

## Scope

This code applies to all project spaces (GitHub repository, issue tracker,
discussions, future chat) and to public spaces when an individual is
representing the project.
